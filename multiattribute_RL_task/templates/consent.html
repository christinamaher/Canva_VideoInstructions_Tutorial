SUMMARY OF THIS RESEARCH STUDY:
In this study, you will be playing a task called Gem Hunters. In this task, you will be presented with a set of gems with unique colors and shapes. On each round there is a special feature, either gem color or gem shape that predicts reward. On each round, your job is to learn through trial and error what the special feature is and use that knowledge to select the gems for which you are most likely to get rewarded. On some rounds you will receive a hint about the special feature and on other rounds you will not. The more rewards you collect, the higher your bonus payment will be at the end. This study will take you approximately 15 minutes.
